Why We Exist

After using several browsers--some of which were good but not perfect--I decided to make my own.

Of course I would, I like making projects that are way too complicated for no reason.

I wanted a browser that I could not only trust--but that I could use daily.

While for a while I used Firefox, I learned they weren't up to much good either, which sucks because I really liked it.
